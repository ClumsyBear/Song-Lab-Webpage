About Us

Welcome to Song Lab's webpage. This lab pursues the development and application of theories and methodologies from Data Science to solve scientific problems arising from medical and public health sciences, in particular from the fields of environmental health sciences and nutritional sciences. People from this lab are strongly interested in interdisciplinary research in the areas of statistics, operation research, and machine learning, with the core interest in the statistical foundation of big data analytics, and with target applications in processing and analyzing big data from various applied sciences, including asthma, environmental health sciences, nephrology, and nutritional sciences.

Data Science has been always playing an important role in the medical and public health sciences. This multi-disciplinary science branch has provided analytic tools and software for data collection, modeling and data analysis, which has helped advance the biomedical research and knowledge to improve human quality of life. Complex big data collected from environmental health sciences and nutritional sciences have presented great challenges to data science for further growth and advancement of statistical method and theory tailored to the needs from both medical and public health sciences.

Song Lab now has two postdoctoral research fellows, six PhD students and two master students.
